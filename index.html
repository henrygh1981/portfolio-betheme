    A UX Designer on a journey to create delightful simple interfaces and interactions that empower users to engage more effectively. I am very curious and interested in new ways to improve how computers and users interact through Design Thinking process.

    User Research

    During individual and group design projects, I conducted multiple user research methods to develop empathy for the users/people that I was designing for. I developed insights into the problem I was trying to solve by conducting interviews, creating personas and decision mapping. Following brainstorming and ideation, I came up with the ideas and created wireframes and interactive prototypes, which I tested with real people to make sure I built user centered design solutions.

    User Interface Design

    Throughout my projects, I have worked on user interface design based on universal and inclusive design, accessibility and usability such that my designs were valuable, useful, and accessible to as many of users as possible. I acquired in-depth and professional level experience in Figma. I have developed moodboards, mockups and style guides. I learned and applied atomic design principles and connected dots to big picture concepts like responsive web design. I have also learned interaction design to bring life to my visual interfaces. I tested and iterated my designs based on usability testing interviews such that my designs are easy to interact for users.
